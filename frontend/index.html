🚀 Quick Start

Get Started in 3 Steps

  1. Go to Interview Tab - Upload your resume

❓ FAQ

Do I need to install anything locally?

No! This web app runs entirely in your browser. Just upload your resume and start interviewing. Your data is only sent to process your interview.

How do I get better feedback?

The more detailed your answers, the better the feedback. Try to elaborate on your experience, provide examples, and speak naturally.

Can I track my progress?

Yes! Check the History tab to view all your past interviews and see your improvement over time.
